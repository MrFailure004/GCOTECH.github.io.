About GECOTECH CLUB

A student-driven innovation club encouraging creativity, teamwork, and real-world engineering skills.

GECOTECH CLUB encourages students to learn by building. Members work on electronics, software, robotics, automation, fabrication and research-based projects. The club regularly organizes workshops, competitions, technical talks, and project demonstrations to improve practical knowledge.
